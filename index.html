Hi, I’m Judy.

I earned a BS in Computer Science and spent a year as a Software Engineer at Target. I loved building, but I wanted more impact at the point where people need it most — healthcare. Now I’m a non‑traditional pre‑med pursuing medicine while still shipping software and research projects that make learning and care delivery better.

This site tracks the journey: study notes, MCAT prep, code snippets, and reflections. I hope it helps anyone who’s considering a big pivot. With focus and community, the leap is possible.

I’m also a mentor and organizer — running CS outreach, leading research committees, and coordinating national panels on AI in Medicine. Good leadership creates better teams; better teams create better care.
